About Me

I’m Romain Lemore, a PhD student at Inria Paris, in collaboration with CASIS a company spetialized in cardiovascular data analysis, particularly MRI. I work within the COMMEDIA team, wich focuses on the numerical simulation of biofluids. My reasearch is supervised by Miguel Fernandez and Damiano Lombardi. My work focuses on reconstructing blood flow within pathological aortas, aiming to determine biomarkers that can assist in the diagnostic of aneuvrysms. The reconstruction is based on 4D-Flow MRI data wich, depite its good acuracy, cannot be used directly for certain biomarkers due to the noise.
